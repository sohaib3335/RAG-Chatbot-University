# Westfield State University Campus Dining Services

**Document Number:** WSU-CDS-2023-005
**Effective Date:** August 15, 2023

## Overview

Welcome to Westfield State University Dining Services! We are committed to providing a wide variety of delicious, nutritious, and convenient dining options to our entire campus community. This document outlines the dining services available, including meal plans, dining locations, and important policies. Our goal is to enhance your campus experience by offering quality food and exceptional service.

Westfield State University offers a variety of dining locations across campus to suit your needs. From traditional dining halls to cafes and grab-and-go options, you'll always find something to satisfy your cravings.

## Dining Dollars

Dining Dollars are a flexible and convenient way to dine on campus. They are included with every meal plan and can be used at any of our dining locations, including the food court and cafes. Dining Dollars work like a debit card, and the amount is deducted from your account with each purchase. Additional Dining Dollars can be added to your account at any time.

## Dietary Accommodations

Westfield State Dining Services is committed to providing options for students with dietary restrictions and allergies. Our culinary team is trained to handle special dietary needs, and we offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options daily. Students with specific concerns should contact our registered dietitian to create a personalized dining plan.

## Guest Meals

Guest meals are included with the Unlimited, 15-Meal, and 10-Meal plans. You can use your guest meals to treat a friend or family member to a meal at The Nest. Simply inform the cashier that you would like to use a guest meal when you swipe your card.

## How to Access and Use Dining Services

Your Westfield State University ID card is your key to accessing all dining services on campus. Simply present your ID card to the cashier at any of our dining locations to use your meal plan, Dining Dollars, or guest meals. You can check your meal plan balance and transaction history online through the university portal.

## Policies and Guidelines

To ensure a pleasant dining experience for everyone, we ask that you adhere to the following policies and guidelines:

*   Your meal plan is non-transferable and can only be used by you.
*   Do not remove food or beverages from The Nest, with the exception of one piece of fruit or a dessert.
*   Please be respectful of other diners and our staff.
*   Clean up after yourself by returning your dishes to the designated area.
*   Report any lost or stolen ID cards to the university police immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I change my meal plan?**

A: Yes, you can change your meal plan during the first two weeks of each semester. To do so, please visit the Dining Services office.

**Q: What happens if I run out of Dining Dollars?**

A: You can add more Dining Dollars to your account at any time through the university portal or by visiting the Dining Services office.

**Q: Do my meal swipes and Dining Dollars roll over to the next semester?**

A: Meal swipes do not roll over from semester to semester. However, any unused Dining Dollars will roll over from the fall to the spring semester, but not to the next academic year.

**Q: What if I have a food allergy?**

A: We are committed to accommodating students with food allergies. Please contact our registered dietitian to discuss your specific needs and develop a plan to ensure you can dine safely on campus.
