# Westfield State University Financial Information

**Document Number:** FIN001
**Effective Date:** 2024-08-01

## 1. Overview and Purpose

This document outlines the tuition and fee schedule for the 2024-2025 academic year at Westfield State University. It is intended to provide students, families, and the university community with a clear and comprehensive understanding of the costs associated with enrollment. All tuition and fees are subject to change without prior notice, upon approval by the Westfield State University Board of Trustees.

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What is the difference between in-state and out-of-state tuition?**

A: In-state tuition is a lower rate for students who are legal residents of the state where the university is located. Out-of-state tuition is a higher rate for students who are not legal residents.

**Q: Are payment plans available?**

A: Yes, the university offers a payment plan that allows students to pay their tuition and fees in installments over the course of the semester. For more information, please contact the Student Financial Services Office.

**Q: What happens if I miss the payment deadline?**

A: A late fee of $100 will be assessed for any payments received after the due date. Students with outstanding balances may also be subject to having their course registration canceled.
